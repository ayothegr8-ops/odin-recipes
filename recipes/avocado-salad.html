Avocado Salad

Description

This avocado salad is creamy, refreshing, and packed with nutrients your body will actually use. It combines healthy fats from avocado, hydration from crisp vegetables, and a bright citrus dressing that ties everything together. It's perfect as a light meal, a side dish or even something you can throw together quickly on the road without needing anything complicated.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime (or lemon)
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

    • Optional Add-Ons

  • 1/2 cup chickpeas or black beans
  • Grilled chicken or shrimp (to make it a full meal)
  • Feta cheese
  • Jalapeño slices
  • Corn kernels

Steps

  1. Prep your ingredients by dicing the avocados, halving the tomatoes, chopping the cucumber and slicing the red onion.
  2. Ad everything to a bowl including the avocado, tomatoes, cucumber, onion and herbs.
  3. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the mixture to keep the avocado from browning and add brightness.
  4. Drizzle olive oil evenly over the salad.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste/
  6. Gently toss everything together, being careful not to mash the avocado.
  7. Add optional ingredients like chickpeas, grilled protein, or feta cheese.
  8. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
  9. Serve immediately or chill slightly, for an extra refreshing bite.
