Grilled Pineapple with Honey & Cinnamon

Description

This grilled pineapple dessert is naturally sweet, lightly caramelized, and packed with tropical flavor. The heat brings out the pineapple's natural sugars, while honey and cinnamon add warmth and depth without makiing it heavy. It's the perfect finish after a savory or spicy meal-refreshing, satisfying, and simple to make whether you're at home or keeping it minimal.

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh pineapple, peeled, cored, and sliced intto rings or spears
  • 2-3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground honey
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter or coconut oil

    • Optional Add-ons

  • Pinch of sea salt
  • Lime juice or zest
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chili powder or cayenne

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium heat
  2. Prep the pineapple by cutting it into rings or long spears, removing the core.
  3. Brush each piece with melted butter or coconut oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Place pineapple on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, until grill marks appear and the fruit begins to caramelize.
  5. Drizzle honey evenly over pineapple while it's still warm.
  6. Sprinkle cinnamon across all pieces for that warm, spiced flavor.
  7. Add optional toppings like lime juice, a pinch of salt, or chile powder if desired.
  8. Remove from heat and let cool slightly for 1-2 minutes
  9. Serve warm and enjoy that juicy, caramelized goodness.
