Mexican Pizza Home

Description

Start by preparing your layers: cook and season ground beef with taco seasoning, and warm refried beans until smooth and spreadable. Lightly fry or bake two flour tortillas until they're crisp but not overly browned—this gives you that signature crunchy base. Spread a layer of refried beans on one tortilla, then add the seasoned beef evenly on top. Place the second tortilla over it like a sandwich, then spread a thin layer of enchilada or taco sauce across the top and sprinkle generously with shredded cheese. Preheat your oven to 400°F (204°C) and place the assembled pizza on a baking sheet. Bake for about 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is fully melted and bubbly and the edges are lightly golden. Once out of the oven, finish it with diced tomatoes, sliced black olives, and green onions for that classic look and fresh bite. Let it cool slightly before slicing so it holds together, then serve and enjoy that crispy, cheesy, layered goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas (medium size)
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade blend)
  • 1/2 cup refried beans
  • 1/2 cup enchilada or taco sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Mexican blend, or both)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (204°C).
  2. Prepare your base by placing one crispy tortilla flat on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread refried beans evenly over the tortilla, creating a smooth layer.
  4. Add seasoned ground beef on top of the beans, distributing it evenly.
  5. Place a second tortilla on tip, pressing gently to form a layered "pizza".
  6. Spread taco or enchilada sauce over the top tortilla in a thin, even layer.
  7. Sprinkle shredded cheese generously across the top.
  8. Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges are slightly golden.
  9. Remove from the oven and let is cool for 1-2 minutes to help it set.
  10. Add fresh toppings like diced tomatoes, sliced black olives and green onions if desired.
  11. Slice and serve warm, enjoying the crispy, layered texture.
